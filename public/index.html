Why rest time?

Rest time gives your pup the opportunity to have some uninterrupted rest. Some pups have a difficult time relaxing with a lot of stimuli. In an effort to make sure the pups aren’t over-exerting themselves, rest time is mandatory.

Boarding

At Woofers & Whiskers, we make overnight boarding a breeze. Each pup will be comfortable and safe in their own suites, which include raised cots, and access to water at all times. You are welcome to bring your pup’s favorite toy, or even their bed for a little bit of home comfort. We also have several larger suites to accommodate multiple pups from the same family (limited availability).

We offer different boarding options, ranging from 1:1 time with staff, offer special treats and geriatric services. Each pup will be fed twice daily (unless otherwise specified by owner).

At the end of the day, our number one priority is that your pup is safe, comfortable and happy while you are away.
